Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-10-18

Data Collection and Usage

This extension does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Store your chat conversations on external servers
  • Track your browsing behavior
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or telemetry

Data Processing

All processing occurs entirely on your device:

  • Chat extraction happens locally in your browser
  • Exported files are saved directly to your computer
  • Export history is stored locally using Chrome’s Storage API
  • No data is transmitted to any remote server

Permissions Justification

  • activeTab: Required to access the current chat page and extract its content
  • downloads: Required to save exported files to your chosen location
  • storage: Required to store your local export history (up to 20 recent exports)

Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any material changes will be communicated via the extension’s update notes.
